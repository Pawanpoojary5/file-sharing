How to Find MAC Address
Windows:
  1. Open Command Prompt
  2. Type: ipconfig /all
  3. Look for "Physical Address"
macOS/Linux:
  1. Open Terminal
  2. Type: ifconfig
  3. Look for "HWaddr" or "ether"
iPhone/iPad:
  1. Settings → General → About
  2. Look for "WiFi Address"
Android:
  1. Settings → About phone → Status
  2. Look for "WiFi MAC Address"
